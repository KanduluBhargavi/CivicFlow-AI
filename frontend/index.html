Core AI Services

Four models, one seamless pipeline

Every complaint moves through the same intelligence stack before it ever reaches a human officer.

01

Language Detection

Identifies the citizen's language automatically from free-form text or voice, across 22 scheduled languages and regional dialects.

02

Translation

Converts every complaint into a standardised working language for officers, without losing local nuance or intent.

03

Department Prediction

Maps complaint content to the correct department — Water, Roads, Electricity and more — with a confidence score attached.

04

Priority Detection

Flags urgency and public-safety risk instantly, so critical cases surface at the top of an officer's queue.

How It Works

The complaint journey

From the moment a citizen submits a grievance to the moment it's closed — fully tracked, fully transparent.

  1. Complaint Submitted

    Citizen lodges a grievance via web, app or IVR in their own language.

  2. AI Classification

    Language, category and urgency are detected automatically, in under a second.

  3. Department Assigned

    The complaint is routed to the correct department and jurisdiction with no manual sorting.

  4. Officer Review

    An assigned officer verifies details and takes ownership, with SLA timers running.

  5. ✓

    Resolved

    The citizen is notified and asked to confirm resolution, closing the loop.
